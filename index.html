About

A Bit About Me

I am a mature and motivated individual with almost 30 years experience within the areas of IT software development and Team Management.

As with many people of "my generation" I fell into IT rather than actively seeking it as a career choice.

Whilst working in Finance in the mid-80s, I discovered the typing pool had a machine called a "Wang" and there was a software package installed called "20/20" - a spreadsheet! Numbers, money, a spreadsheets...

To me it instantly clicked that the spreadsheet could help me with the accounts I was currently processing on a day-to-day basis.

When the IT department found out I had put a (rudimentary) system together they offered me a job and the rest, as they say, is history.

Howdy!

I have had the opportunity and luck to live and work in several major cities around the world: Sydney, New York City, London and Barcelona.

I have worked for both public and private sectors, vendors, suppliers and consumers.

I have had the opportunity to work on micro, mini, mid-range and mainframe computer hardware and a plethora of operating systems, and many shrink-wrapped, customised and proprietary software packages.

I’ve held roles of Project Manager, PMO, Business Analyst, Software and Production Support Specialist, Quality Assurer, Production Support Manager. The only role in the “IT job families” that I’ve not held – to date – is that of developer.

After leaving the workforce at the beginning of 2014, I took a sabbatical and studied a number of things that were of interest: Personal Training, Rehabilitation Sports Massage Therapy and Spanish.

After relocating and settling in beautiful Barcelona, I started to have doubts about retiring so early and decided to take on new course of study – Web Front End Developer!

To put my newly acquired knowledge to work and fund my ongoing desire to learn I am actively looking for a position that will help me improve and continue to grow my new skills to work.
